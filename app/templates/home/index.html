How to Play

To play the maze Game, simply navigate through the maze using the arrow keys on your keyboard.The objective is to reach the exit while avoiding obstacles and collecting power-ups along the way.

Is is free?

Yes, the Maze Game is free to download and play. However, there may be in-app purchases available for additional features or content.

Yes, you can Customize your charater in the Maze Game. There are various skins and accessories available that you can unlock or purchase.
